## 📊 Motorola Sales Analytics Dashboard

A Power BI dashboard project analyzing mobile sales data to uncover revenue drivers, customer behavior patterns, and business insights.

---

## 🧠 Problem Statement

Businesses often collect large volumes of sales data but struggle to extract meaningful insights that can guide decision-making.

This project aims to analyze mobile sales data to answer key business questions:

* Which brands and products drive the most revenue?
* How do sales vary across time and regions?
* What patterns exist in customer purchasing behavior?

---

## 📁 Dataset

* Source: Mobile Sales Dataset (Excel)
* Size: ~10,000+ records
* Features include:

  * Brand, Mobile Model
  * Sales Amount, Quantity
  * Transaction Details
  * Payment Method
  * Geographic Location

---

## ⚙️ Tools & Technologies

* **Power BI** – Dashboard creation & visualization
* **Excel** – Data preprocessing
* **DAX** – KPI calculations (basic measures)

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

* **KPIs:**

  * Total Sales: 47M
  * Transactions: 234
  * Average Value: 40K
  * Quantity Sold: 1K

* **Interactive Filters:**

  * Month
  * Brand
  * Mobile Model
  * Payment Method

* **Visualizations:**

  * Sales trends over time
  * Brand-wise performance
  * Product distribution
  * Customer ratings
  * Geographic sales map

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

* **Revenue Concentration:**
  A small group of brands (Samsung, Xiaomi, OnePlus) contributes to the majority of total revenue.

* **Product Dependency:**
  A limited number of products generate a disproportionate share of sales, indicating potential risk.

* **Sales Patterns:**
  Sales fluctuate across days, with noticeable mid-cycle peaks suggesting demand trends.

* **Payment Behavior:**
  Cash dominates transactions, indicating low adoption of digital payment methods.

* **Regional Trends:**
  Sales activity is concentrated in specific geographic regions, highlighting opportunities for targeted expansion.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Focus marketing efforts on top-performing brands and products
* Expand underperforming regions using targeted campaigns
* Encourage digital payment adoption for better tracking and efficiency
* Use sales trends to optimize timing of promotions and campaigns

---

## 🧠 Key Takeaway

Data is only valuable when it helps identify what drives revenue and where to focus next.
